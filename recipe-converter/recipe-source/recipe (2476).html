Cockles marinière on garlic toast

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

Serves 1

By Lesley Waters
From Ready Steady Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 200g/7oz cockles
  • 100ml/3½fl oz white wine
  • 30g/1oz butter
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped
  • 3 slices farmhouse bread, toasted

Method

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan, add the onion and gently fry for four minutes.

  2. Add the cockles and white wine, cover with a lid and leave for 3-4 minutes or until all the cockle shells have opened.

  3. Discard any that have not opened or the shells that are damaged.

  4. Add the butter and garlic to the pan and cook for one minute.

  5. To serve, place the toasted bread onto a serving plate. Pour the cockles and sauce over the bread.
