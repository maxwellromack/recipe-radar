Aperitivo di passata

Preparation time

less than 30 mins

Cooking time

less than 10 mins

Serves

4

Recommended by

1 person

Dietary

 

Vegetarian

Try making this Italian-style virgin cocktail to serve before a dinner party – the lime, chilli and tomato hit the spot.

By Gino D'Acampo
From Saturday Kitchen

Ingredients

  • 300m/10fl oz passata sauce
  • 2 limes
  • ¼ nutmeg, grated
  • 3 pinches of sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of chilli powder

Method

  1. Place the passata, lime juice, nutmeg, salt and chilli powder in a large bowl and stir well.

  2. Place in the fridge.

  3. Get a cold glass and slightly wet the rim. Dip into the sugar.

  4. Pour the mixture into the glass and decorate with a slice of lime. Serve chilled.
