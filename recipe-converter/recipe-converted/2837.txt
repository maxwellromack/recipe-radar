

Coconut pudding with cream



Preparation time
less than 30 mins


Cooking time
10 to 30 mins


Serves
Makes 1


Dietary

 
Vegetarian








By Lesley Waters


From Ready Steady Cook






Ingredients

butter, for greasing
50g/1¾oz butter
50g/1¾oz caster sugar
50g/1¾oz fresh coconut, grated
75g/2¾oz self-raising flour
25g/1oz cocoa powder
2 free-range eggs
50ml/2fl oz double cream




Method


Preheat the oven to 200C/400F/Gas 6.


Grease a small loaf tin with butter.


Place the butter, sugar, grated coconut, self-raising flour, cocoa powder and eggs into a food processor and blend to make a loose dough.


Spoon the mixture into the greased loaf tin and place in the oven to bake for six minutes, or until golden and cooked through.


Remove from the oven and turn out onto a plate.


To serve, cut the cake into thick slices, place on plates and drizzle with cream.
