Title: Carpaccio of tuna and tomato with lime and sesame
Prep time: Less than 30 mins
Cook time: No cooking required
Serves: 1
Author: Gino D'Acampo

Ingredients:
1 tbsp olive oil
1 lemon, juice only
1 lime, juice only
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
100g/3Â½oz very fresh raw tuna, thinly sliced
2 tomatoes, thinly sliced
1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
1 tbsp sesame seeds
salt and freshly ground black pepper

Instructions:
Place the olive oil, lemon and lime juice and balsamic vinegar into a bowl and mix well.
Add the tuna to the bowl and let the fish marinate in the bowl for ten minutes.
To serve, arrange the tomato slices on a clean plate and place the tuna on top of the tomato. Sprinkle the fresh parsley and sesame seeds over the top and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.
